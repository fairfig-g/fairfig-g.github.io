About us

Gabriele Sani

Founder

Fair Fig provides finely taylored support to our customers.

We do not try to fit our technology of choice to all projects that we come across. It all starts with you.

We listen to your your needs and targets, and then we research state-of-the-arts solutions that would fit you to present you some alternatives. You choose the one that works best for you, and then we assemble a team of experts in the technologies and tools needed to deliver it.

We have chosen to work in this way since we want to keep your perspective first, but also because technology changes all the time. With this approach, we can guarantee you that the team following you is made of true experts in the field.

This gives us greater flecibility, too. While you will always have a single point of contact, behind the scene we can taylor the support based on your needs and budget. From 24/7 support and development to low-cost alternatives, and the flexibility to quickly ramp up or freeze resources if your needs change over time.

This is our promise. It is always professional, and personal.
